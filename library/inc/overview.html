Book Fine Overview
Important End-of-the-Year Library Notice:

The end of the year is quickly approaching. It will soon be time for students to return their library books, class novels, and textbooks. Students are receiving overdue/fine notices weekly. We encourage students to please take care of library obligations now, to avoid long lines at the end of the school year.


All Library books are due on or before Tuesday, May 28. If students have lost any books or incurred fines, they must pay with CASH ONLY; school district policy states that we cannot accept checks after
May 10.


To receive yearbooks on time, students must clear all library obligations and previously incurred textbook obligations no later than 1:00 p.m. on Friday, May 30th.


Seniors must clear all library and textbook obligations and return all current textbooks by 1:00 on Thursday, June 6th, to participate in the graduation ceremony without any special clearance.


9th - 11th grade students must return their current textbooks by 1:00 on Wednesday, June 12th, to avoid potentially having their registration packet and locker assignment withheld for next year. There will be only 3 days during the summer for students to take care of any library/textbook obligations after this date.
