Mobile Apps
Destiny Quest App

Search the SSHS Library Catalog and access your library record. Students may now access the SSHS library catalog from Apple or Android device. To download this free app:

  • Go to the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and type in Destiny Quest, and download. The app looks like this:
  • Type in the library location: destiny.simi.k12.ca.us.
  • Select Santa Susana High School.
  • Type in your user name and password. Your user name is your district id number, and the password is the first initial of your first name followed by your entire last name (ie. John Smith's password is jsmith).
  • Once you have successfully downloaded this app, this is what you will see (example of Android app):
  • There are several choices you may select. You may click on the icons, or:
    • To search the library catalog for a book, just type the title into the search box.
    • Click on the "Account" icon to view your library records.
    • You can even reserve a book and then pick it up at the library.


The Library Without Walls Goes Mobile

Students may now access all the resources of our Library Without Walls database program from their Apple or Android device. To download this free app:

  • Go to the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and type in: accessmylibrary-school edition. The app looks like this:
  • Download this free app.
  • You will need to select your state, your city, and finally your school.
  • Enter our password: susana.
  • Click on Access My Library.
  • Click on Update my Resources.
  • Click on Access My Library again.
  • Click Gale databases.
  • The next time you open this app, you will be able to access all of our Gale databases.


SSHS Library Media Website

Please download the QR Code for the Santa Susana Library Media Center web site as well. This will give you quick access to all of our library web resources, including the school style guide, recommended web sites, AP history study guides and access to our Reading Counts eCatalog.
